Rasmalai Recipe

Ingredients

For the Rasgullas (Paneer Balls):

  • 1 litre full cream milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 3–4 cardamom pods

For the Rabri (Milk Syrup):

  • 1 litre full cream milk
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 4–5 cardamom pods, crushed
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • 8–10 chopped pistachios
  • 8–10 chopped almonds

Instructions

  1. Prepare Paneer (Chenna): Boil milk, curdle with lemon juice, strain, wash, and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Make Rasgullas: Knead paneer, shape into balls, cook in sugar syrup for 15 minutes.
  3. Prepare Rabri: Boil milk till reduced, add sugar, cardamom, and saffron.
  4. Assemble Rasmalai: Squeeze syrup from rasgullas, add to rabri, garnish with nuts, and chill before serving.

Ready to Serve: Delicious Rasmalai!
